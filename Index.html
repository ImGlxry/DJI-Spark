What is a drone?

A drone is a type of aircraft that can be remotely controlled using a controller or a software that has the same function as a controller. Drones are unmanned aircraft meaning there is no pilot, crew or passenger on board and they rely on their embedded system which work with a GPS and in conjunction with sensors.

About DJI

SZ DJI Technology is a popular aircraft organisation that solely manufactures unmanned aircrafts (drones). This company offer camera drones and other products that are within the spectrum they are known for their best-selling drones in the market which are DJI Mini 3, DJI Mavic 3, DJI Mavic 3 Classic, DJI Avata, DJI Mini 3 Pro and DJI Air 2S.
